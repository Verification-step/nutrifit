General

Nuttifit is an all-in-one health and wellness app that combines nutrition tracking, personalized workouts, AI coaching, and progress analytics in one simple, beautiful experience.

We'll offer a generous free tier with core features, plus a premium plan for advanced AI coaching, detailed analytics, and exclusive programs.
